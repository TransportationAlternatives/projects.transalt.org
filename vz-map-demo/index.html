About this site

Transportation Alternatives mapped every Vision Zero fatality between January 1, 2014, and December 31, 2023.

What is an “other motorist”? This category was created by the New York City Department of Transportation in 2021 that includes victims riding standing e-scooters and moped-style devices that cannot be legally defined as e-bikes, e-scooters, or motorcycles. The victims were previously categorized as pedestrians, bicycles, or motorcycles, depending on specific type.

What is Vision Zero Priority Corridor? These are corridors identified by DOT with the most pedestrians killed or seriously injured per mile.
