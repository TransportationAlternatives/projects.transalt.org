Show your support for congestion pricing in 2 minutes or less

Right now, the MTA is collecting public comments on congestion pricing. Here’s why you should care:

  • The MTA is reviewing all comments — and we need to make our voices heard. We know a minority of New Yorkers who oppose congestion pricing are working overtime to curtail the program.
  • This is why we need to submit clear comments: Congestion pricing now with no exemptions. Each new exemption will lead to higher tolls for everybody else.
  • The future of New York City depends on the future of congestion pricing. Congestion pricing means faster buses, cleaner air, fewer cars, and safer streets.
  • Congestion pricing will fund much-needed upgrades to our transit system, including the Second Avenue subway, the Interborough Express, train and subway station accessibility projects, new subway signals, and more.

We'll help you write a public comment

We need you to submit a comment in support of congestion pricing. This will only take a minute or two, but it is vital to ensuring the success of the program.
